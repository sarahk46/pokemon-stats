The Pokédex Visualizer

Looking for the tool to help you become the very best Pokémon trainer like no one ever was?

How about to catch 'em all? Well, look no further than the Pokédex visualizer!


The Pokédex visualizer is much similar to your actual Pokédex, which is a digital encyclopedia that gives information about

all known Pokémon. Unlike the Pokédex, where entries include extraneous information, like the weight, footprint, and location, the Poké dex visualizer shares instead the most relevant information for you to be truly the very best. To select specific generations of Pokémon, select from the checkboxes.

Then, to choose among the different Pokémon types, click the colors on the legend (shift-click to get multiple types!).

And voila! You can see the Pokémon of interest and their speed, attack, defense, and HP on the plot below!

You can also hover over the points to identify specific Pokémon and learn more about them.
